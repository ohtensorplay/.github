## 👋 Welcome to ohtensorplay

**ohtensorplay** is an open-source organization focused on tensor computation, deep learning infrastructure, and high-performance AI tools.

### 🚀 What we do

- Build efficient tensor operation libraries
- Develop training & inference frameworks
- Explore novel neural network architectures
- Share reproducible research and benchmarks

### 🤝 Get involved

We welcome contributions, ideas, and collaborations. Feel free to explore our repositories and join us in advancing open-source AI.
